Who I

This is Rishikesh — a CS student from Madurai, just finished my first important part my life (school) ,stepping into the unknown unpredictable world only with the hope and trust on me and figuring out what comes next. I got into tech because I was curious about how things work behind the screen. Not just using software, but understanding it. Building it.
